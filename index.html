What is support?

  • Efficiently maintaining updates for Drupal core and contributed modules
  • Cultivating a healthy relationship with clients
  • Setting and maintaining realistic expectations, goals, and deadlines
  • Effectively tracking budget allocation and development resources

When does support become relevant?

  • Support is ALWAYS relevant..
  • Plan early for support in any new project
  • Discovery time is strongly encouraged

Who's responsible?

Project Management

  • Set expectactions early in the relationship
  • Maintain open and concurrent communication
  • Never make assumptions about client requests
  • Effective Requirement Gathering
  • Know your team and know your limitations

Who's responsible?

Development Operations

  • Custom Modules vs. Contributed Modules
  • Development workflows and release cycles
  • Maintain an active and healthy code repository
  • Follow best practices and coding standards
  • Development and staging environments for testing deployments

How do you plan for support?

  • Plan for future use cases
  • Schedule deployments
  • Development and staging environments
  • Quality Assurance Testing
  • Task/Project documentation
